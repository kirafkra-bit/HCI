Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new task?
Go to Task Manager and click "+ Add Task". Fill in name, subject, and due date, then click Save.
How do I switch to dark mode?
Click "Dark Mode" at the bottom of the sidebar, or toggle it in Profile under App Preferences.
Can I edit or delete a task?
Yes — each task row has edit and delete buttons. A confirmation prompt prevents accidental deletion.
How are notifications managed?
Visit the Notifications page to dismiss, mark as read, or clear all. Filter by type using the tabs.
Is PŌLARIS accessible?
Yes — full ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, and WCAG 2.1 AA color contrast throughout.
